# Template: Executive Strategy Briefing

## Use case
C-suite/board-level strategic initiative briefing (3-5 year outlook, investment decision support)

## Slide structure
1. **Cover** - Title, date, confidentiality
2. **Situation** - Current state, market context, competitive landscape
3. **Strategic Challenge** - Key decisions needed, constraints, dependencies
4. **Proposed Direction** - Recommended strategy, key pillars (3-5 max)
5. **Investment Summary** - Budget, timeline, resource requirements
6. **Key Success Factors** - Critical milestones, metrics, risks
7. **Next Steps** - Decision needed, timeline, stakeholders
8. **Appendix** - Supporting data, detailed financials

## Speaker notes template

**Slide: Situation**
- Open with market shift or competitive threat
- Quantify the gap (revenue impact, market share, capability gap)
- Connect to company strategy/vision
- Call to attention: "This requires a decision in the next 30 days"

**Slide: Strategic Challenge**
- Frame as a choice, not a problem
- Outline constraints (budget, timeline, skills)
- Ask rhetorical question: "How do we..."
- Pause for engagement

**Slide: Proposed Direction**
- Lead with outcome/benefit first
- Explain each pillar with 1-2 supporting points
- Acknowledge trade-offs ("This requires us to...")
- Visual dominates; text is minimal

**Slide: Investment Summary**
- Lead with total cost of ownership
- Break down by phase or category
- Connect to expected ROI/benefit
- State confidence level ("Based on X analysis")

**Slide: Next Steps**
- What decision is needed? (Yes/No, Choose A/B/C)
- By when?
- Who needs to approve?
- What happens if we delay?

## Timing guide
- 1 min per slide = 8 minute deck (good for executive update)
- 2 min per slide = 15-17 minute deck (good for decision meeting)
- Target: 12-15 minutes with Q&A

## Visual notes
- 1 visual per slide (chart, diagram, photo)
- Maximum 3 lines of text per slide
- Dark blue/teal background (executive style)
- Bold sans-serif headlines (44pt+)
